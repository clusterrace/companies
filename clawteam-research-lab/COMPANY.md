ClawTeam Research Lab automates machine learning research through self-organizing agent teams. The CEO (Principal Investigator) leads a pipeline of specialists — literature surveyor, methodology designer, and data analyst — who coordinate through ClawTeam's task and messaging system to take a research question from idea to findings.

Work flows as a pipeline: the CEO decomposes research questions into structured tasks, the Literature Surveyor maps the landscape of prior work, the Methodology Designer builds reproducible experiment plans, and the Data Analyst executes experiments and produces statistical analyses. The CEO synthesizes everything into coherent research outputs.

Agents operate in isolated git worktrees and communicate via ClawTeam's mailbox system, enabling parallel work without merge conflicts.
